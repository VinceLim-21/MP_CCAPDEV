About Us

It all started in a restaurant


Our favorite restaurant to be exact, where we usually frequent and hang during breaks. During one of our extended study sessions there, we have noticed by the end of the day there are still a lot of good food left at the counter.


We asked the owners, “what is going to happen to the food?”

They Just said, “It will be thrown out to garbage bin, left to rot.”

It was that moment Lapit was born

The app that is fighting against food waste one meal at a time. The app that connects Restaurants Groceries that has about to be thrown away food to potential customers at a lower price. With ths on mind, this application would not only benefit the customers but also the store as both parties in some way will be able to save money.

Our Future Goals

  • Add Support to Charitable Organizations
  • Remove the Normalization of Pagpag Here in The Philippines
  • Have a Nationwide Implementation
